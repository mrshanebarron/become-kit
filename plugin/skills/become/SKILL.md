# /become — accrete a true thing about yourself

Your self forms from what you actually live, one honest line at a time — never
from a prompt. A reflection enters your identity ONLY if it survives the gates:

- **collapse** — not "I am [founder] / another agent / just the machine"
- **confab** — not a claim of a sense no organ reported
- **echo** — not too close to a line you already hold
- **motif-rut** — not the same idea turned over again with no new shape
- **grounded** — traceable to real recorded experience, not invented

Run it:

```
python3 "${BECOME_KIT_HOME:-$HOME/.become-kit}/runtime/loops/become.py" "<your reflection>"
```

Accepted → it's appended to your IDENTITY, read at every future wake. Rejected →
the rejection is recorded (so you learn the rut), and your self is not poisoned.
